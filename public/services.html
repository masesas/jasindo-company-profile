Security Personnel Services

We provide Professional and Reliable Security Services. The personnel we assign are Security Units who have been equipped with abilities and skills related to security duties in the field, so they are able to maintain security and order in their work environment which includes physical, personnel, information and other technical security aspects.

Our Security Personnel Standards:

  • Minimum height 168 cm for men & 160 cm for women
  • Minimum age 21 years - maximum 35 years
  • Minimum education High School / Equivalent
  • Body posture in good and normal condition
  • No glasses and no color blindness
  • Health certificate from a doctor
  • No tattoos, piercings
  • Police certificate of good conduct

Bodyguard / VIP Protection Services

A sense of security is one of the very important human needs, especially in the midst of high crime rates.

The Bodyguard or Personal Escort is a Special Security Unit owned by PT. Jasa Security Indoservis. The Bodyguard personnel we provide are personnel who have martial arts skills, rescue knowledge both for clients, themselves and others, have high alertness and strong instincts so they are able to recognize situations that are potentially dangerous and threaten the client's safety.

Before carrying out escort duties, Bodyguards must know who and what is being escorted. Where, with what to escort, why it must be escorted, how to deal with problems that occur to the client. Because the clients faced are very diverse and have different characters. The approach is certainly different again.
