About Jasindo Security

Professional security services since 2016

Company Overview

PT. Jasa Security Indoservis (Jasindo Security) is a company engaged in the provision and management of labor services (Outsourcing Service), especially security personnel services or Satpam.

In carrying out its business activities, PT. Jasa Security Indoservis is supported by experienced and professional experts with backgrounds from Professionals, Military and Police. We have experience managing security in various locations including Offices, Factories, Warehouses, Mining, Executive Housing, Educational Places, Entertainment Places throughout Indonesia with various conditions and regional characteristics.

Our Vision

To become a security service company that prioritizes quality and provides maximum benefits to stakeholders.

Our Mission
